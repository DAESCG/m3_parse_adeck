List of Models used in ADECKs

Full Document at RAL

This section attempts to provide a comprehensive and searchable listing of the TECH designators of all TC guidance aids, both past and present. Links provide more information about a particular model, including which organization contributed, and documentation about that model.

Data Sources

This list was compiled from the following sources:

  • NHC's techlist of all current TECH's that can appear in NHC's public a-decks:
    ftp://ftp.nhc.noaa.gov/atcf/docs/nhc_techlist.dat

  • NHC's techlist of all deprecated TECH's that no longer appear in NHC's public a-decks:
    ftp://ftp.nhc.noaa.gov/atcf/docs/nhc_techlist.dat.deprecated

  • JTWC's techlist of all current TECH's that can appear in JTWC's a-decks (not public):
    http://www.nrlmry.navy.mil/atcf_web/docs/database/new/techlist.dat.pearl

  • The Tropical Cyclone Modeling Team (TCMT)'s 2012 HFIP Demonstration Page - regional models section
